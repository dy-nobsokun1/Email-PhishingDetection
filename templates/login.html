How to Generate App Passwords

For Gmail Users:

  • 1. Go to your Google Account and select "Security".
  • 2. Under "Signing in to Google," select "App Passwords".
  • 3. You may need to sign in again. Choose "Mail" as the app, and "Windows Computer" as the device.
  • 4. Google will generate a 16-character app password. Copy it.

For Outlook Users:

  • 1. Go to your Microsoft Account and select "Security".
  • 2. Under "Advanced security options," find "App passwords" and click on "Create a new app password".
  • 3. Microsoft will generate a new app password. Copy it.

Use this app password in the "App Password" field above instead of your regular email password.
