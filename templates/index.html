How Does It Work?

Our system leverages cutting-edge machine learning algorithms and natural language processing (NLP) techniques to analyze various aspects of emails, including content, metadata, and attachments. Here’s how we protect your inbox:

  • Email Content Analysis: We scan the email text for patterns and keywords commonly associated with phishing attempts.
  • Header Inspection: Our system examines the email headers to detect spoofing and other malicious activities.
  • URL and Attachment Scanning: All links and attachments are checked against a database of known threats and scanned for viruses or malware.
  • Real-Time Updates: Our detection models are continuously updated with the latest threat intelligence to ensure maximum protection.
